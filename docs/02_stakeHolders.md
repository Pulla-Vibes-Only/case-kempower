## Step Into Their Boots

### Hotel guests

**What matters most?**  
Lights are convenient to use  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“Is the system actually easy enough for me to use?”  
**Key question?**  
“Does this really elevate my hotel experience?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Honest feedback

### Hotel workers

**What matters most?**  
The system is reliable  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“Is the system really easy for guests to use?”  
“If problems occur, can we handle them?”  
**Key question?**  
“Does this system not bring us more work?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Honest feedback and new ideas  

### Other hotel users

**What matters most?**  
The system benefits us/them  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“Can we use the system to make our event good?”  
**Key question?**  
“Does the system bring enough value to us?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Honest feedback and new ideas  

### Other hotel services

**What matters most?**  
The system is able to build the right mood for our business  
The system brings customers to the hotel who visit our business  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“Can we actually set up the lights so that the customers enjoy their time?”  
**Key question?**  
“Does the system bring enough value to our service?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Honest feedback and new ideas  

### Financial (marketing, budget)

**What matters most?**  
The system brings enough value to attract customers cost efficiently  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“What if this is a wrong investment?”  
“Does this help us advertise our hotel?”  
**Key question?**  
“Does the system bring enough value to cover the investment?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Money  

### Hotel owner

**What matters most?**  
The system helps guests enjoy their stay at our hotel  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“What if this is a wrong investment?”  
**Key question?**  
“Does this actually benefit us and our guests?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Approval  

### Investors and financiers

**What matters most?**  
The system brings enough money for the hotel to pay back the investment  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“What if the hotel goes bankrupt?”  
**Key question?**  
“Can we really trust this system?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Approval  

### Suppliers

**What matters most?**  
They deliver the parts as expected  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“What if there is a shortage of suitable parts?”  
**Key question?**  
“Can we deliver everything reliably?”
**What do we need from them?**  
Supplies, reliability  

### Public authorities

**What matters most?**  
The system is safe to use  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“Does the system explode under heavy use?”  
**Key question?**  
“Can we really trust this system?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
Approval

### PullAVibes Oy Ab

**What matters most?**  
The system is safe to use  
**What keeps me up at night?**  
“Can we deliver a reliable, functional and advertisable product?”  
**Key question?**  
“Is the customer satisfied with the system we have delivered?”  
**What do we need from them?**  
References, money  

## Interview questions for stakeholder

* What are your hotel’s most important values?
* What kind of things do you value in your hotel’s every day operations? (efficiency, atmosphere, transparency…)
* Are there some issues you are facing with your current lightning?
* Is there any feedback you have received from your guests lately that we could help fix?
