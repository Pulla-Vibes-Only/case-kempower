## FAQ

**Will this application give the customer the power to dim the lights in their hotel room?**  
The system lets the visitor adjust the lighting however they want. Brightness, temperature, color… You name it!

**What if the visitor is an absolute noob with tech?**  
Our UI is made very clear and easy for the user. However, we also have an automated user mode. Just let the system decide!

**How does the system save energy?**  
First, the system knows when there is somebody in the room and turns the lights on. When the room is empty, the lights are off. Simple as that!

**How scalable is the system?**  
From tiny motels to gigantic hotel complexes (even ones with confusing corridors).
